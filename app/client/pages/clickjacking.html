Clickjacking is also known as UI dressing, where the attacker tricks users
into clicking things that were hidden over visible objects. It can be done
by embedding the target website in an iframe and making it invisible, or
by simply turning a hidden object's opacity to 0. The goal of the user in
this bug would be to identify where the clickjacking is happening and
disable it before submitting the feedback.
